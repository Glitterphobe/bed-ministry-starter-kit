# Operational Playbook — Outline

> **Status: scaffold.** Chapter structure and interview questions are ready;
> the content is captured in interview sessions with the San Antonio founder,
> one chapter at a time. A chapter is "done" when a new-city leader could act
> on it without calling San Antonio first.

The starter kit's software gets a city taking requests and tracking inventory
on day one. This playbook covers everything the software *doesn't*: how to
build beds, source materials, recruit and screen volunteers, stay legal and
insured, and find the families who need you.

## How content gets captured

Each chapter folder contains:

- `README.md` — the chapter itself (template with TODO banner until captured)
- `interview-questions.md` — the questions the founder answers to fill it

Capture process: pick a chapter → answer its interview questions (voice notes,
a call transcript, or written answers all work) → draft the chapter from the
answers → founder reviews → remove the TODO banner.
