# Interview Questions — Sourcing & Vendors

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. For each catalog category (frames, mattresses, bedding, hardware, comforters, bears/books): built, bought, or donated — and from whom?
2. Which vendors do you use for mattresses, and what do the four SKUs (6"/8" x 38x74/39x75) cost at your volume? Any negotiated pricing a new city could reference?
3. Where do comforter sets come from? Why the with-sheets/without-sheets distinction?
4. What are the current book titles per age tier and where do you buy them? Same for bears.
5. What lumber grade/species do you buy and where? Typical cost per frame?
6. What donated items do you refuse (used mattresses? used bedding?) and why?
7. Lead times: if inventory hits zero on an item, how long to restock?

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
