# Interview Questions — Cost Model

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. All-in, what does one twin bed delivered cost (frame materials + mattress + bedding + extras)? One bunk? One crib setup?
2. What did San Antonio spend to get started, and what would you buy first if starting over with $5,000? $20,000?
3. What minimum inventory floor do you keep before you feel comfortable accepting requests?
4. What are the recurring monthly costs (storage, insurance, fuel, consumables)?
5. What costs surprised you — things a new city won't think to budget for?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
