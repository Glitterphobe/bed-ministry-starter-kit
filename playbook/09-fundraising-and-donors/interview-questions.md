# Interview Questions — Fundraising & Donors

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. How is San Antonio funded — church budget, individual donors, grants, events? Rough proportions?
2. Do you pitch a 'sponsor a bed for $X' number? What is X and how did you set it?
3. How do build-day events work as fundraisers — who pays for what?
4. How do you report impact back to donors (the dashboard endpoint, the monthly report)?
5. What fundraising flopped? What would you tell a new city to try first?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
