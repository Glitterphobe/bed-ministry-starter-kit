# Interview Questions — Volunteers & Screening

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. List every recurring volunteer role and roughly how many people fill each in San Antonio.
2. Which roles require a background check, and which provider/process do you use? Cost per check?
3. How did you find your first ten volunteers? What recruiting works now?
4. What does a new volunteer's first day look like for each role?
5. What's the minimum crew to run the ministry at all (the skeleton crew)?
6. How do you handle a volunteer who shouldn't be around kids or shouldn't drive?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
