# Interview Questions — Referral Partnerships

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. List your referral sources and roughly what share of requests each sends.
2. How did the first school-district or caseworker relationship start? Who made the introduction?
3. What is CarePortal and how does a new city plug into it (or its local equivalent)?
4. What are your eligibility criteria? Have you ever declined a request, and on what grounds?
5. What does the tracking number (Care Portal/APN#) mean in your system, and what should a city without CarePortal use?
6. If a new city has zero referral relationships, what are the first three doors to knock on?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
