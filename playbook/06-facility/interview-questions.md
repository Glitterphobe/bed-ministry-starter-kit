# Interview Questions — Facility

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. Describe your current space: square footage, how it's divided, what you'd change.
2. How are mattresses stored (they're the bulkiest item)? Comforters? Frames?
3. Is the storage physically organized to mirror the inventory categories? Does that make the monthly count faster?
4. What are the minimum viable and comfortable square footages for a new city?
5. Any hard lessons — moisture, pests, theft, borrowed space that fell through?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
