# Interview Questions — Delivery Logistics

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. Walk me through one delivery start to finish: from 'status = Packed' to driving away.
2. What vehicles do you use and how many beds fit per trip? Trailer specifics?
3. How many people on a delivery team, and what does each do at the home?
4. How do you schedule with the caregiver — and what happens when nobody answers the door?
5. What happens in the home beyond assembly (prayer, bear/book presentation)? What's your photo/privacy policy?
6. How do the pick-list PDF and delivery-date field actually get used on the day?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
