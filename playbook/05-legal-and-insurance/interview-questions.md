# Interview Questions — Legal & Insurance

Answer these however is easiest (written, voice memo, call transcript). The
chapter gets drafted from the answers, then reviewed before the TODO banner
comes off.

1. How is San Antonio structured legally — under the church, or separate entity? What would you recommend to a new city and why?
2. What insurance do you carry (general liability, auto, other) and what did securing it involve?
3. Do volunteers sign anything? Do families sign anything at delivery? Can we include your forms as samples?
4. What is your child-safety policy in practice (two-adult rule, who enters the home, etc.)?
5. Has a lawyer reviewed any of this? What legal questions should a new city ask locally?

---

*Answers can be partial — a chapter can ship at 80% and improve when a new
city asks the question you skipped.*
