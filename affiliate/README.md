# Affiliate Model — Addenda List (not yet written)

The starter kit assumes **independent replicas**: a new city takes everything
freely, runs under its own name, and owes nothing back. That works today and
requires none of the documents below.

If the founder later chooses a formal **affiliate model** (cities operating as
"Child of God Bed Ministry – [City]" with shared branding and standards), the
package would additionally need:

1. **Name & brand license + style guide** — who may use the name/logo, how,
   and what ends that permission. (Note: the noncommercial code/doc licenses
   do not govern naming at all; today the README simply says new cities pick
   their own names.)
2. **Affiliate agreement** — minimum standards (child-safety compliance,
   background checks, data care), reporting expectations, and termination
   terms.
3. **Central reporting spec** — each city's public-dashboard JSON endpoint
   already exposes aggregate impact numbers, so a national roll-up is nearly
   free: a registry of city endpoint URLs plus a small aggregator.
4. **Shared legal/insurance review** — whether affiliates need aligned
   coverage, and who bears liability for whom.
5. **Onboarding / certification checklist** — what a city must demonstrate
   before flying the shared flag.
6. **Trademark decision** — whether to register the name before licensing it.

None of this blocks a city from starting today as an independent replica, and
an independent city could convert to affiliate later.
